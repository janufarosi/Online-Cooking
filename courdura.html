Indian Cooking Course Duration

Week 1: Introduction to Indian Cooking

  • Understanding regional cuisines of India
  • Essential spices & pantry setup
  • Kitchen tools for Indian cooking
  • Assignment: Prepare Jeera Rice & Basic Dal

    • Week 2: Indian Spice Mastery

  • Spice families: seeds, powders, whole vs roasted
  • Making spice blends: Garam Masala, Chaat Masala
  • Tempering (Tadka) techniques
  • Assignment: Prepare 2 spice blends & use them in a simple curry

    • Week 3: Indian Breads & Rice

  • Roti, Paratha, Poori, Naan basics
  • Rice dishes: Pulao, Lemon Rice, Tamarind Rice
  • Assignment: Cook Paratha + Raita

    • Week 4: Lentils & Curries

  • Dal Tadka, Dal Fry, Sambhar
  • North vs South Indian gravies
  • Assignment: Cook a Dal Curry + Chutney

    • Week 5: North Indian Cuisine

  • Paneer Butter Masala, Rajma, Chole Bhature
  • Non-veg: Butter Chicken / Rogan Josh
  • Street food: Golgappa (Pani Puri), Aloo Tikki
  • Assignment: Cook Paneer Butter Masala

    • Week 6: South Indian Cuisine

  • Breakfast: Dosa, Idli, Vada, Upma
  • Main dishes: Chettinad Chicken, Malabar Fish Curry
  • Rice: Bisi Bele Bath, Curd Rice
  • Assignment: Cook Masala Dosa + Coconut Chutney

    • Week 7: East Indian Cuisine

  • Bengali: Shorshe Ilish, Aloo Posto, Rasgulla
  • Odisha: Dalma, Pakhala Bhaat
  • Street food: Puchka, Egg Roll
  • Assignment: Cook Rasgulla or Sandesh

    • Week 8: West Indian Cuisine

  • Gujarat: Dhokla, Thepla, Undhiyu
  • Rajasthan: Dal Baati Churma, Gatte ki Sabzi
  • Maharashtra: Misal Pav, Puran Poli
  • God: Vindaloo, Bebinca
  • Assignment: Cook Dhokla

    • Week 9: Snacks & Street Food Special

  • Samosa, Pakora, Pav Bhaji, Bhel Puri
  • Assignment: Make Samosa + Chutney

    • Week 10: Indian Desserts

  • Gulab Jamun, Kheer, Jalebi, Malpua, Modak
  • Festival sweets: Gujiya, Ladoo
  • Assignment: Make Gulab Jamun

    • Week 11: Beverages & Festive Foods

  • Masala Chai, Filter Coffee, Lassi, Jaljeera
  • Festive thalis (Diwali, Eid, Onam, Pongal)
  • Assignment: Cook Onam-style Mini Thali

    • Week 12: Advanced & Fusion Indian Cooking

  • Plating Indian food for fine dining
  • Fusion: Butter Chicken Pasta, Paneer Tacos
  • Final Project: Prepare a Full Regional Thali of choice (North/South / Bengali)
